What Secguard does

It turns device sales into a protected lifecycle.

A company receives phones, syncs them from ERP, registers each device, detects risky movement, enables customer protection, and handles theft recovery from one controlled environment.

Four core engines

Built as a real platform, not a single tracking screen.

01

ERP Integration Engine

Syncs inventory, sales, branches, staff, receipts, warranties, and ownership state from the existing ERP.

02

Device Intelligence Engine

Maintains device registry, lifecycle status, risk profile, telemetry history, and last-known security state.

03

Fraud Detection Engine

Flags unsold active phones, duplicate IMEIs, suspicious staff patterns, mismatched receipts, and stock anomalies.

04

Customer Protection Engine

Allows customers to enroll devices, report theft, view warranty, receive alerts, and follow recovery progress.
