FAQ

Built for real rollout questions.

Does it work with MAPPHEX or another ERP?

Yes. Secguard is structured as a security intelligence layer that receives inventory, sale, branch, receipt, and warranty events through ERP webhooks or scheduled sync.

Does the customer app require special hardware?

No. The mobile client uses consent-based app telemetry, location permission, SIM/network events, and protected account workflows.

Is tracking continuous or on demand?

Tracking should be policy-based and consent-driven. Normal protection can use last-seen updates, while theft cases can increase monitoring frequency.

What happens if a device goes offline?

The platform keeps the last known location, SIM state, timestamp, device history, and recovery case status until the client reports again.
