Platform modules

One security layer. Four engines.

Secguard connects ERP events, device intelligence, fraud rules, and customer protection into a system that can be tested, debugged, and extended module by module.

Device Intelligence

The registry is the heart of the platform.

IMEI, serial number, model, branch, ERP status, activation status, SIM changes, owner, warranty, telemetry, and risk score stay tied together.

ERP Connector

Business truth in.

Inventory, sales, receipts, branch transfers, warranties, staff, and customer records arrive through webhooks or scheduled sync.

Fraud Detection

Rules that catch leakage.

Unsold active phones, duplicate identifiers, receipt gaps, SIM abuse, and branch mismatch become prioritized alerts.

Live Tracking

Last-seen intelligence.

Location pings, network type, carrier, battery, SIM hash, and timestamps feed maps and recovery workflows.

Customer Protection

Recovery starts cleanly.

Customers register devices, report theft, view warranty, and follow recovery progress without calling five departments.

Audit + Reports

Operational truth out.

Every action writes an audit trail and exports reports for managers, officers, and compliance review.

Data flow

From ERP event to fraud action.

1ERP sends event

2Device profile updates

The registry refreshes ownership, branch, status, and risk context.

3Telemetry arrives

The mobile app or API sends consent-based device events.

4Rules evaluate

Fraud logic detects mismatches and suspicious device movement.

5Officers act

Alerts become investigations, recovery cases, reports, or audit events.
