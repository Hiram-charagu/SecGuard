About Secguard

Built for retailers that need device security connected to real operations.

Secguard extends an ERP ecosystem with device intelligence, customer protection, fraud detection, and recovery workflows. The goal is simple: every phone should have a clean lifecycle from stock entry to sale, ownership, protection, and recovery support.

A calm command layer for serious security work.

The platform is designed around tenant isolation, branch-aware permissions, audit-ready actions, and consent-based mobile monitoring. It should feel organized enough for enterprise teams and clear enough for small retailers growing into better controls.

ERP-firstInventory and sales remain the business source of truth.
Device-awareIMEI, serial number, warranty, ownership, telemetry, and risk live in one registry.
Recovery-focusedThe system helps customers and security teams respond after theft reports.
Privacy-awareMonitoring is transparent, permissioned, and limited to legitimate protection workflows.
