About Secguard

We make every phone accountable.

Secguard exists because phone retailers need more than inventory records. They need to know when a device moves, activates, changes SIM, enters a theft case, or becomes evidence in a fraud investigation.

Device lifecycle secured

Stock entry - sale - customer protection - recovery - audit trail.

01ERP-first

Your ERP stays the business source of truth. Secguard adds the security layer around it.

02Device-aware

IMEI, serial, warranty, branch, owner, SIM events, telemetry, and risk live in one record.

03Recovery-focused

Theft reports turn into tracked recovery cases, not scattered messages and paper notes.

04Privacy-aware

Monitoring is consent-based, role-limited, transparent, and designed for legitimate protection.

Operating model

A calm command layer for serious security work.

Secguard is structured around tenant isolation, branch-aware permissions, audit-ready actions, and mobile telemetry that can support real investigations without becoming invasive surveillance.

Company adminsControl branches, staff, ERP sync, settings, and reports.
Security officersAssign, escalate, dismiss, and close high-risk alerts.
InvestigatorsWork recovery timelines, SIM events, and device evidence.
CustomersRegister protected devices and follow theft recovery status.
